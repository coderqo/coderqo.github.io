Bi 1: S Ayza
S Ayza l s bnh phng ca mt s nguyn t: 4 = 2^2, 9 = 3^2;
Cho dy s nguyn dng N hy m xem trong dy c bao nhiu s
Ayza, v tm s Ayza nh nht khng c trong dy.
M t u vo:
- Dng u ch s nguyn dng N (1<=N<=10^5).
- Dng th 2 cha N s nguyn dng Ni ngn cch nhau 1 du cch
(1 <= Ni <= 10^18).
M t u ra:
- Dng u l s lng s Ayza trong dy.
- Dng hai l s Ayza nh nht khng thuc dy.
Rng buc:
- C 20 % s test N < 10^3, Ni < 10^9.
- 80 % s test c N >= 10^3, Ni >= 10^9.

Bi 2: Anh Lp chm ch
Trng THPT Quc Oai sp t chc cuc thi chn i tuyn thi HSG TP
tin hc ca trng. Lp l mt hc yu thch tin hc chun b cho
k thi, Lp tham gia CLB CODER QO, CLB tin hc ca trng c

anh trong CLB cho lp chin lc luyn tp nh sau: Ngy u tin
mt bi, ngy th 2 hai bi,... Ngy N gii N bi. Cc anh chun
b cho Lp mt danh sch gm n bi khc nhau vi s lng bi tp
ln lt l a1, a2, a3,...an. Nu ngy th i Lp c th chn ra c
mt c s lng bi tp ln hn hoc bng i th Lp s gii thnh
cng , ngc li nu ngy th i lp khng th chn c c
s lng bi tp ln i th lp s phi tm dng luyn hc thm
kin thc mi. Hy cho bit Lp c bao nhiu ngy luyn tp trc k
thi.
M t u vo:
- Dng u tin cha s nguyn dng N l s lng cc anh
chun b cho lp (1 <= N <= 10^5).

bi tp trong th i.
M t u ra:
- Mt s nguyn dng duy nht l s ngy Lp s luyn tp.
Rng buc:

- 80 % s test cn li khng c rng buc g thm.

Bi 3: Nghin cu
Thnh ph PioTech l mt thnh ph bin. Nhn thy mc nc bin c
s thay i sau nhiu nm. chun b cho vic ng ph thin tai,
ch tch thnh ph PioTech ng Trn Minh Dng c ngi theo di
s thay i ca mc nc. ng T nh Lc l ngi c kin thc su
rng, ng sng trong qun 11a1 ca thnh ph PioTech. 11a1 l qun
hng chu nhiu thit hi nht do s bin i nc bin hng nm.
Vi tm lng yu thng dn, yu qu hng. ng T nh Lc may mn
c ng Trn Minh Dng c i nghin cu s bin i mc nc. Vi
s thng minh ca minh ng Lc nhanh chng pht hin thy quy
lut thay i lng nc rng: mc nc bin ca mt nm bt k bng
trung bnh cng mc nc bin ca nm trc v nm sau. Da vo
nghin cu ca ng Lc. ng lc yu cu ng Trung Bi tnh mc nc
bin nm th N.
Ui khng may my tnh ca ng Trung Bi u khng th lu c cc
s ngoi s 0 v 1. Bn hy gip ng Trung Bi biu din mc nc
bin nm th N di dng nh phn v ghi kt qu theo th t ngc
li.

- Dy s nh phn ngc li ca mc nc bin nm N.
Rng buc:

Bi 4: Khong cch
Coder Quc Oai l mt vng quc giu p v nhiu ti nguyn gi
tr. V l mt vung quc tuyt vi nn Coder Quc Oai thng b dm
ng. Vit Hong v Trng Tun l cc t tng ca vng quc Coder
Quc Oai. bo v t nc ca mnh c an ton nht Tun v
Hong cng nhau a ra mt tng l xy dng cc khu qun s
trn khp t nc. Khi bt u xy dng Tun nhn thy kinh ph
xy dng mt khu qun s rt tn km. bo v c khp t nc
trong phm vi kinh ph th cc khu qun s phi cch xa nhau nht
c th.
Bn hy gip Tun - Hong xy dng M khu qun s sao cho khong
cch gia hai khu qun s lin tip cch xa nhau nht c th.
M t u vo:
- Dng u tin cha 2 s nguyn dng N v M (s to c th
xy dng c khu qun s v s khu qun s cn xy).
2<=N<=10 ^5, M <= N;
- Dng th 2 cha N s nguyn dng ai: a1, a1,... an phn tch
nhau bi du cch. ai <= 10^9.
M t u ra:
- In ra khong cch nh nht gia hai khu qun s lin tip
trong phng n ti u va tm c.
Rng buc:
- 20% s test: 1<=N, M <= 10.
- 80% s test cn li khng c rng buc g thm.
